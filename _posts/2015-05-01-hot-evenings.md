the air is warm and the air is still  
the concrete walls, the tarmacked streets  
breathe in  
breathe out  
no wind to hear, no hugging self  
and only laughter  
the drunken rancour  
old men slurping icecream  
people disbeleving  
and nature hears the sounds of a city
