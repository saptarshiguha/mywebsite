A letter to a restless 11 month old boy on a hot summers day

{% poem %}
Summer air sitting still  
Sunny boy does not   
Summer cat in  deep sleep  
Sunny boy not  
Heavy air, supine cats  
Move move move!  
Dearest tashi, please not?  

{% endpoem %}
